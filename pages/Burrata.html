Grilled Peach Burrata

This bright summery dish is the perfect appetizer for a dinner party and just to devour as fast as possible

Ingredients

  • 2 whole Peaches
    • Frozen is also fine just make sure to throughly pat them dry
  • 4 ounces of Burrata
  • 11⁄2 cups of Cherry Tomatoes
  • 8 ounces of Basil
  • 1⁄4cup of olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

💡 Tip: This is a recipe all about personal prefrence, adjust these numbers as you see fit!

Directions

  1. Slice peaches into 2 inch slices and set aside
  2. Add a light amount of oil on a grill, or grill pan, and place the peaches on the pan, making sure not to stack
  3. Well the peaches grill, julienne the basil and half the cherry tomatoes
  4. flip the peaches off the pan when grill marks are visible on the bottom. Remove from pan and place on a large plate
  5. Place tomato halves over the peaches as well as jullienned basil.
  6. place the burrata on top and drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  7. Serve with crusty bread and enjoy!!
