Seared Lamb Chops

A meaty dish with a refreshing Tzatziki sauce and salad is a great entree for any dinner party or just for your own enjoyment

Ingredients

  • 6 medium lamp chops
  • 5 cups arugula
  • 2 blood oranges
  • 2 cups fennel
  • 3 cups olive oil
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cup yogurt
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 cups dill
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Chop the cucumbers and dill (don’t need to be even; will be blended)
  2. Large dice the blood orange in chunks as evenly as possible.
  3. Juice and zest the lemon
  4. In a food processor combine the yogurt, cucumbers, dill, lemon juice and zest, salt and pepper. Blend usinging the pulse function,
  5. Season the lamb chops with salt and pepper.
  6. Add oil to cast iron pan and heat over medium heat until you can feel the heat by hovering your hand over the pan
  7. Place the lamb chops in the cast iron pan.
  8. Sear one on side for approximately 6 minutes and the other for approximately 4 minutes.
  9. While lamb chops are cooking, combine the fennel, arugula, and blood orange in a bowl.
  10. Make a vinaigrette with some olive oil, vinegar, salt and pepper, and lemon juice.
  11. Add the vinaigrette to the salad and mix!
  12. Now Plate- Place lamb chops down together, put cucumber salad on side, put tzatziki on other section of plate and drizzle a little over the plate.
