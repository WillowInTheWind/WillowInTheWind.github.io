Citrus-y Scallop Crudo

A citrusy fresh dish that is a perfect dish to share with a crowd, each component combines together to make a bite to die for

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Coconut Milk
  • 2 Blood oranges
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 Naval Orange
  • 1 pound of Scallops
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 green onion
  • 1⁄4 cup Chili Oil
  • 6 pieces of Sushi Ginger

Directions

  1. Halve the scallops and place at the bottom of a shallow bowl
  2. In a measuring cup combine the juice of the Orange, 1 blood orange and half of the other, 1⁄2 lemon and mix until a light pink, almost pepto bismol coloured liquid
  3. Pour the liquid over the scallops until just the tops of the scallop are visible
  4. top with pieces of sushi ginger, sliced shallot, sliced blood orange and lemon, and a drizzle of chilli oil
